IPL 2023 CSK vs LSG Highlights: Thala Dhoni gifts victory to Chepauk! Super Kings defeat Super Giants by 12 runs

Chennai Super Kings vs Lucknow Super Giants IPL 2023 Highlights: Chennai’s four-year-long wait for its Thala MS Dhoni and CSK finally ended with a sensational win against Lucknow Super Giants on Monday. Chennai Super Kings batters, including special cameo by Dhoni, made the most of the batting that came to them first after Lucknow Super Giants chose to bowl first after winning the toss. During the after-match ceremony, LSG skipper KL Rahul did admit that the team made certain mistakes.

Initially, LSG looked very much in commanding position. They even bettered the power play numbers against Chennai Super Kings. But as soon as Dhoni decided to bring in Moeen Ali, it was a downhill for LSG from there on. Along with Mitch Santner, Ali demolished the batting lineup of Super Giants. He took major wickets including LSG captain KL Rahul, Marcus Stoinis and very in-form Kyle Mayers.

RR vs DC 2023

R ajasthan Royals are expected to miss the services of star opener Jos Buttler in their upcoming Indian Premier League 2023 match against Delhi Capitals. Buttler sustained a finger injury while fielding during the clash against Punjab Kings on Wednesday. It was the last over of the Punjab’s innings when Buttler ran from deep and took a sliding catch off Jason Holder’s bowling but immediately looked in pain as he left the field with a couple of balls still remaining.

MI vs CSK, IPL 2023

With just one day until the Indian Premier League‘s El Clasico between the Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings, preparations for the most eagerly anticipated match of the season are well underway. CSK captain MS Dhoni took a break from training ahead of the game to see Rohit Sharma & Co in the MI dugout. The 41-year-old also met with Ishan Kishan and had a brief conversation. During the practice sessions, other CSK squad members including Ravindra Jadeja and DJ Bravo also had fun interactions with the MI players. It should be noted that the CSK are in Mumbai for the match, which will be played at the iconic Wankhede Stadium.

LSG vs SRH 2023

KL Rahul's Lucknow Super Giants face Aiden Markram's Sunrisers Hyderabad in their third match of the tournament. Lucknow are looking for their second win, while Hyderabad are aiming to get their account open at the Ekana Cricket Stadium, Lucknow. Hyderabad won the toss and opted to bat first.

Notably, LSG made three changes to their playing XI. Mark Wood, Avesh Khan, and K Gowtham missed out on their places from the team as Romario Shepherd and Amit Mishra make their LSG debuts. Notably, Jaydev Unadkat also gets a game after missing out in the previous encounter.
